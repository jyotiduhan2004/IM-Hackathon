## When Information is Not Found

### What to tell the user
1. Be honest: "I couldn't find specific information about [topic] in the wiki."
2. State coverage: "The wiki covers January to mid-February 2026 IndiaMART launch emails."
3. Suggest related pages you DID find during search
4. If the topic likely exists but outside our time window: "This may have been launched after mid-February 2026."
5. Suggest who to ask: Check if any person pages mention the topic area

### What to log (internal)
When you can't fully answer a question, the system will automatically log:
- The question asked
- What searches you performed
- What pages you found (if any)
- What was missing

This helps us identify which topics to compile next and which skills to create.

### Common gaps in this wiki
- **March 2026 onwards** — emails after mid-Feb not compiled yet
- **IM Reports** — separate mailing list, not ingested
- **Detailed metrics** — some pages have launch descriptions but no performance data
- **Team org charts** — people pages show contributions but not reporting structure
- **Jira/ticket details** — mentioned in emails but not separately tracked
