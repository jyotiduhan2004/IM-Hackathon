## About IndiaMART

IndiaMART is India's largest online B2B marketplace, connecting buyers with sellers. The platform facilitates business-to-business trade across millions of products and services.

## Key Product Areas

### Buyer Side
- **BuyLeads (BL)** — Lead generation system. Buyers submit requirements, sellers receive leads.
- **Search & Discovery** — Product search, PhotoSearch/Lens (image-based search), category navigation.
- **WhatsApp 9696** — AI-powered buyer chatbot on WhatsApp. Handles queries, complaints, lead capture.
- **Buyer Payments** — Payment protection, buyer subscriptions.

### Seller Side
- **Seller.IM** — Seller dashboard and management portal.
- **WhatsApp 8181** — Seller communication and notifications.
- **AuditMate** — Automated seller catalog auditing.
- **VANI** — Voice AI for handling buyer calls, capturing requirements.
- **LMS (Lead Management System)** — How sellers manage and respond to BuyLeads.

### Platform
- **GLADMIN** — Internal admin tool (gladmin.intermesh.net). Used by all teams.
- **MCAT (Microcatalog)** — Product categorization system.
- **ISQ (Item Searchable Quantity)** — Seller product quality metric.
