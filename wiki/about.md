# About

This wiki is compiled automatically from emails on our internal mailing
lists. An LLM-driven pipeline ingests each thread, synthesizes durable
knowledge into topic / system / policy / entity pages, and renders the
result through MkDocs Material.

- **Source of truth** — the `raw/` email corpus.
- **Synthesis** — `scripts/compile_all.py` produces `wiki/*.md`.
- **Viewer** — this MkDocs site.

See `docs/issues/09-internal-wiki-structure.md` and
`docs/issues/10-phase1-implementation-plan.md` for the design plan.
